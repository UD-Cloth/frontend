
# Men's Fashion E-Commerce Store
## Inspired by The Souled Store | Red & White Premium Theme

### Overview
A modern, premium men's clothing e-commerce website with a bold red (#DC2626) and white color scheme. The store will feature a clean, professional design optimized for the Indian market with full e-commerce functionality powered by Shopify.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation & Core Shopping Experience

### 1.1 Homepage
- **Sticky header** with logo (center), search bar, wishlist icon, cart icon, and user account
- **Hero banner carousel** with full-width promotional slides for collections and offers
- **"New Arrivals" section** - horizontal scrollable product cards with image, name, price
- **Category showcase** - visual tiles for T-Shirts, Shirts, Hoodies, Jackets, Jeans, Accessories
- **Trending/Featured collection** banner
- **Trust badges strip** - Free Shipping, Easy Returns, COD Available, Secure Payments
- **Footer** with navigation links, contact info, social media, and newsletter signup

### 1.2 Product Listing Page (Category Page)
- **Grid layout** with product cards (4 columns desktop, 2 columns mobile)
- **Filter sidebar** - Size, Color, Price Range, Collection
- **Sort dropdown** - Price low/high, Newest, Popular
- **Quick view** on hover showing additional product images
- **Pagination** or infinite scroll

### 1.3 Product Detail Page
- **Image gallery** with thumbnail navigation and zoom functionality
- **Product info** - Name, price (with discount strikethrough), rating stars
- **Size selector** with size guide popup
- **Color variants** as clickable swatches
- **"Add to Cart"** and **"Add to Wishlist"** buttons (red CTA)
- **Product details accordion** - Description, Fabric & Care, Size Chart
- **Customer reviews section** with star ratings
- **"You May Also Like"** - related products carousel

---

## Phase 2: Shopping Cart & Checkout

### 2.1 Shopping Cart
- **Slide-out cart drawer** for quick access
- Product thumbnails, name, size, color, quantity selector
- Remove item option
- **Price summary** - Subtotal, shipping estimate, total
- "Proceed to Checkout" button
- **Continue Shopping** link

### 2.2 Wishlist
- Grid of saved products
- "Move to Cart" functionality
- Remove from wishlist option
- Share wishlist feature

### 2.3 Checkout Flow (via Shopify)
- **Guest checkout** or account login option
- Shipping address form
- Shipping method selection
- **Payment options** - Credit/Debit Cards, UPI, Net Banking, COD
- Order summary with promo code field
- Order confirmation page with tracking info

## Design System

### Colors
- **Primary Red:** #DC2626 (buttons, accents, CTAs)
- **White:** #FFFFFF (backgrounds)
- **Dark Gray:** #1F2937 (text)
- **Light Gray:** #F3F4F6 (secondary backgrounds)

### Typography
- **Headings:** Inter or Poppins (bold, modern)
- **Body:** Inter (clean, readable)

### Components
- Red primary buttons with white text
- Subtle hover animations
- Card shadows for depth
- Rounded corners (8px radius)
- Smooth page transitions

---

## Technical Approach

**Frontend:** React + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS
**E-Commerce Backend:** Shopify Integration (handles products, payments, orders, inventory)
**Authentication:** Shopify customer accounts
**Payments:** Shopify Payments (Cards, UPI, COD for India)

---

## Implementation Priority

| Priority | Feature | Why |
|----------|---------|-----|
| 🔴 High | Homepage + Navigation | First impression, brand identity |
| 🔴 High | Product Listing + Detail Pages | Core shopping experience |
| 🔴 High | Cart + Checkout | Revenue generation |
| 🟡 Medium | User Authentication | Customer retention |
| 🟡 Medium | Wishlist | Engagement & return visits |
| 🟡 Medium | Search + Filters | Product discovery |
| 🟢 Later | Order Tracking | Post-purchase experience |
| 🟢 Later | Admin Panel | Store management |

This plan starts with the essential shopping experience and adds features incrementally, allowing you to launch quickly and enhance over time.
